The Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Pune is a premier institute dedicated to research and teaching in the basic sciences. Established with the dream of enabling students to shape the future of humanity by inventing novel and sustainable solutions for societal problems, IISER Pune provides a vibrant and intellectually stimulating environment for students at various levels of their academic careers.

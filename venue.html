Travel Information

By Air

Pune International Airport (PNQ) is about 12 km from IISER Pune. Taxis and auto-rickshaws are available at the airport. Prepaid taxi counters are recommended for convenience and safety.

By Train

Pune Railway Station is approximately 7 km from IISER Pune. Prepaid taxis and auto-rickshaws are available at the station. The campus is easily accessible by road from the station.

By Road

IISER Pune is well connected by road. Use Google Maps for accurate driving directions. Ample parking is available for visitors on campus.
